Global assistance to suit your needs

Euro-Center is a global emergency medical assistance company owned by the Munich Re. We service remote and urban corporate companies, governmental organizations, ministries of foreign affairs and expatriate insurance companies Euro-Center is recognized for ‘making the impossible possible’ in emergencies. With 50 years’ experience, Euro-Center is well suited to service ADIPEC participants with solutions and commitment. We have global experience, including in hot spots across Africa and the Middle East.

Global presence and local assistance

Euro-Center provides local presence, having extensive geographical knowledge of local areas and healthcare systems. Three 24/7 medically staffed call centers in Prague, Bangkok and Helsinki, and regional offices in Istanbul, Cyprus, Beijing, Bangkok, Prague, Cape Town, Miami, Sao Paulo, Moscow and Sydney ensure access to all parts of a the world and global coverage.

We speak over 40 languages and have multiple 24/7 language platforms.
